Our Retro Journey

1985 - The Beginning

In a quaint corner of town, Cafe Delight first opens its doors, offering coffee, pastries, and the promise of warm memories.

1995 - Growing Strong

Our iconic Veggie Pizza and Strawberry Milkshake became customer favorites. We expanded to two more cities.

2005 - A New Look

We revamped our cafe with a fresh retro vibe, blending nostalgic elements with modern-day comfort.

2015 - Serving Smiles

With a dedicated team and loyal customers, Cafe Delight became a weekend hangout spot for all foodies!
